Visual Computing Lab · Poznań, Poland

Simulating the natural world,
teaching machines to see it.

We are a research group at the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, Adam Mickiewicz University. Our work spans physically based simulation, generative models, synthetic data, and computer vision — from the architecture of trees to the dynamics of wildfires and storms.
